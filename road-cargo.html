logivalley Surface Cargo is an economical, door-to-door, ground distribution service to over 21,100 locations in India. It offers a cost-effective logistics option for your less time-sensitive shipments, with the following value-added features and benefits:

a) Time-bound Delivery - A fleet of vehicles run to pre-determined schedules to provide committed delivery. Check the Transit Time for information on delivery time, or Contact us.

b) FOV (Freight on Value) - Insurance Arrangement. logivalley can facilitate arrangements to insure your goods through an insurance company. In an eventuality of the shipment being damaged or lost while in transit, logivalley would assist you in the claim process and ensure the claim is settled in the minimum possible time after shipper/consignee handover required documents to the insurance company.

c) Single-window Clearance - Shipments are picked-up from your location, transported to their destination, processed through the regulatory channels and delivered to the consignee. Check the Regulatory for the regulatory requirements to clear your shipment, or Contact us for further information.

d) Real-time Information - On the status of your shipment at all stages of transportation, through our advanced tracking system. You may Track your shipment through the net, or Contact logivalley.

e) Time-Definite Delivery - Check the Transit Time to confirm the delivery time for your shipment, or Contact logivalley.

f) Track your Shipment - Over the net, or Contact us to receive information on the status of your shipment. You can also view, download and print copies of Proof of Deliveries and Delivery Challans of logivalley Surface line shipments.

g) Free Proof of Delivery on Demand - You may use Tracking, or Contact us for information on date and time of delivery as well as name of recipient. You can also view, download and print copies of Proof of Deliveries and Delivery challans of Apex shipments

h) Flexibility - logivalley Apex offers options of Airport-to-Door and Door-to-Airport delivery services, apart from a Door-to-Door service.

i) Economical - logivalley Apex offers a cost-effective logistics solution with time-bound deliveries that provide cost-savings in warehousing and inventory. Price Finder will provide you with the applicable tariff for your shipment.
